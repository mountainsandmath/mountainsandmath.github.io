About Me

I created this site as a space to bring together two things I care deeply about: mathematics and the mountains. It serves as a place to document my learning journey as I continue to grow in math, and to share my thoughts, experiences, and interests along the way.

I also hope this can be a resource for my students; to show that I am a real person, and that mathematics can be interesting and deeply connected to everyday life.

My mathematical interests are broad, with particular curiosity in representation theory, algebraic geometry, and number theory. I am currently focused on developing my mathematical pedagogy. Outside of math, I enjoy learning Mandarin, exploring etymology, philosophy, speedcubing, baking, and trail running.
